Hello, I am In-ah, a graphic designer based in Sydney.

I have spent my youth in three countries(and some more travelling). Coming from this background, my interestes evolves around the topic of in-between-ness, stories unheard, messed up context and rediscoveries of everyday mundane objects. My recent experiments focused on the interaction in print design, exploring the role of audience in a new light - as a collaborator.

I have recently completely by six-year-long study of B.Design in Visual Communication and B.International Studies in Italian at University of Technology, Sydney.
